Julia's Banana Bread
---------------------


Servings: 24

Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup sugar
2 eggs, beaten
3 bananas, finely crushed
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 tablespoons white sugar



Directions:

1. Mix together yeast, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 cup flour, and 1/4 cup warm water. Allow to stand for 30 minutes.
2. Remove odd pots and pans from oven.
3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
4. Add eggs and crushed bananas. Combine well.
5. Sift together flour, soda and salt.
6. Add to creamed mixture.
7. Add vanilla.
8. Mix just until combined. Do not overmix.
9. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan.
10. Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes.
11. Cover wok with lid and steam buns over boiling water for 15 minutes.
